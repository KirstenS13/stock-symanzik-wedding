About the Couple

David and Mikayla are two TCKs (Third Culture Kids) who met online on eHarmony in 2017! A Third Culture Kid is someone who’s lived in several countries (not their parents’ home countries or the country of their nationality) throughout their growing-up years. David was born in New Zealand to British parents, spent his childhood in the Netherlands, then moved to Wisconsin, USA for his middle, high school, and college years. Mikayla spent her childhood in Michigan, USA, then moved to the Philippines for middle and high school, and a few college years, too.

David and Mikayla are both introverted, but love adventures, coffee, bike rides, quiet time, game nights with friends and family, and exploring new places. They currently live in Milwaukee, WI.

David has a Masters degree in Bronze Age Aegean Archaeology from the UK’s Sheffield University, and a Bachelor's degree in Archaeology from the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee (UWM). Mikayla is currently working on her Secondary English Education degree at UWM, and wants to be a high school English teacher. David currently works at the Milwaukee Public Museum as a Visitor Services Representative, and Mikayla works at UWM as a Group Exercise Instructor, a Help Desk Consultant, and a Writing Center Tutor.

Fun Fact: Their first online date happened over Skype, where they quoted Princess Bride to each other and mock-planned a Princess Bride-themed wedding.

Attending Online

What is a livestream?

A livestream is a video setup that allows viewers to witness the video as it is being recorded. What this means is that any of our guests navigating to this page will be able to witness, at the exact same time as our in-person attendees, the ceremony unfold. You will get to see all the funny, heartfelt moments at the same time as those sitting with us in Michigan - albeit from the comfort of your own home.

How do I use it?

Step 1:

Go online and find your way back to this page about 10 minutes before 4:00pm on Saturday, June 13th, 2020.

Step 2:

Using your mouse, scroll to the bottom of this page. You should see a box - at this moment it shows a picture of our cats, Edmund (Black) and Lucy (Ginger) - when we start recording the livestream video will replace our cats
